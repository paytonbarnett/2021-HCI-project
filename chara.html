My Favorite Characters

Home Game Information

There are 23 Habiticians, including Dr. Habit himself. Here are my favorite six:

*tip: mouse over the polaroids to learn more about them!*


Parsley Botch is a businessman and the son of another Habitician, Jimothan Botch. He is awkward and uneasy, coming from a difficult household and having a poor relationship with both parents.

Ronbo is a clown suffering a recent breakup with a fellow Habitician, Tiff Webber. He is in charge of the carnival within the Habitat.

Trencil Varnia is the father of Nat Vancy, who is also at the Habitat. He is worried about their relationship and the fact that she hasn't said "I love you" since she was 12. He might be a vampire.

Kamal Bora was Dr. Habit's dental assistant, but after an incident has quit and resides on the rooftop patio. All he wants is a toothbrush.

Gillis Soco is the bouncer for the lounge, and once given the password he can be found in the carnival wanting a teddy bear from one of the games.

Tim Tam is known around the Habitat for causing trouble and stealing small items from the others. Their speech is blunt and they have a laundry list of tasks for the player to accomplish.
