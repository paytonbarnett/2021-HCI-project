Smile For Me was released on May 31, 2019. It's been described as "an experimental adventure game" by IndieCade and "an unconventional Point-and-Click" by the game's Steam page.


The game is available to download on Steam or Itch.io

Day Lane and Yugo Limbo are the creators of Smile For Me.

Day Lane is a self-described game designer at heart who studied Information Science and loves to code.

Yugo Limbo's inspiration comes from Eastern Europe and American cartoons from the 50s-60s.
